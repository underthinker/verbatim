Verbatim turns your voice into clean, polished text in whatever app has focus.
Press a hotkey, speak, and the words appear where your cursor is - in your editor, your browser, your chat window, anywhere you can type.

Everything happens on your own machine.
Your speech is transcribed locally, polished locally, and typed locally.
No audio, no transcripts, and no usage data ever leave your computer - Verbatim has no telemetry and no cloud account.

## How it works

1. You press the dictation hotkey and start talking.
2. Verbatim records from your microphone while you hold (or after you toggle) the key.
3. When you finish, it transcribes what you said with an on-device speech model.
4. It optionally polishes the raw transcript - fixing filler, punctuation, and casing - with a small on-device language model.
5. It types the result into the app you were using, or falls back to your clipboard if it can't type directly.

## Why local-only

- **Privacy.** Dictation often contains the most sensitive things you write - messages, notes, credentials-adjacent text. None of it is transmitted anywhere.
- **Works offline.** No network is required after the one-time model download.
- **No account, no subscription, no rate limits.**
